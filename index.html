MONTHLY ROADMAP // SEMESTER EXAM PREP
MONTH 01
Foundation build
  • Cover full syllabus once for every subject, chapter by chapter
  • Rebuild core math: linear algebra, probability, calculus basics for AI
  • Practice robotics fundamentals: kinematics, sensors, control systems
  • Start a running formula and concept notebook per subject
  • Solve previous years' question papers to map weak areas
MONTH 02
Applied practice
  • Implement small ML models: regression, classification, basic neural nets
  • Build or simulate one robotics mini-project end to end
  • Do timed subject-wise mock tests, one per weekend
  • Group study or discussion sessions for tough topics
  • Clear pending lab records and assignments
MONTH 03
Revision and lock-in
  • Full syllabus revision using notes, not textbooks
  • Attempt 2-3 full-length mock exams under exam conditions
  • Revise high-weightage and previously weak topics only
  • Prepare a one-page formula and diagram sheet per subject
  • Rest, sleep well, and taper study load in the final 2 days
